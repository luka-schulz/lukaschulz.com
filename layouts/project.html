The Creation of Adam

“Make your own tools.” —﻿Bruce Mau

I have never found the act of learning to be difficult. The difficulty has always come from making the content meaningful. While taking Computer Science I & II, I had a hard time imagining how I could apply what I was learning to my design career. It wasn’t until I began exploring p5.js that I realized how I could apply my skills to something more creative. One thing led to another, and I found myself Photoshopping the face of the New Media Program Chair so that it could be used in my coding experiments. The following page highlights the things I have learned from playing around with code. A special thanks is due for Adam Smith for tolerating my antics.
