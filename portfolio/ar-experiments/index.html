AR Experiments

Experimental, UX/UI

What follows are a series of short vignettes exploring what the future might look like when Mixed Reality becomes ubiqutos. Some pieces are more complete than others but all of them come from a place of fear—for the times we live in—as well as a a place of hope—for what the world might look like if technology were used more empathetically. I truly believe that Augmented (AR) and Mixed Reality (MR) have the power to userpor the smartphone and usher in a new era of computing, one that liberates humanity from screens and allows for interactions that are more humane. For a more in depth musing on Augmented Reality, I suggest reading my thoughts located in this link: An Era of Distributed Computing.
